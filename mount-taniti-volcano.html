Mount Taniti Volcano

Description

Hike up Mount Taniti for breathtaking views of the island and the Pacific Ocean. Guided tours are available for a safe and educational adventure.

Activities

  • Hiking trails for all skill levels.
  • Guided tours with expert guides.
  • Photography spots with panoramic views.

Tips

  • Wear sturdy shoes and bring water.
  • Best visited during the dry season (April to October).
